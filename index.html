TCCT conf - 清交電腦技術討論會

被稱為科技重鎮的新竹，並有清交兩所名校座落於此，應該要有自己的conference。 透過分析開發框架，以及分享實戰經驗，啟發清交學生對程式的熱忱。 發展社群，增進技術的分享，進一步與世界接軌。

We should have our own conference in Hsinchu, a city famous for hi-tech industry and two prestigious universities, NCTU and NTHU. Inspire the students' passion for information technologies, by analyzing the framework of development and sharing practical experience. Develop the community, promote technology sharing and link with the world.
